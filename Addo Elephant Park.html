Addo Elephant National Park is not only a haven for wildlife enthusiasts but also emphasizes conservation efforts, providing a unique opportunity to witness elephants in a protected and thriving environment. Learn about the local Xhosa culture and heritage with guided tours, offering insights into the history and traditions of the region. Enjoy the flexibility of self-drive safaris, allowing visitors to explore the park at their own pace. Well-maintained roads and viewpoints offer excellent opportunities for wildlife observation. Addo is a birding paradise with over 400 species of birds, including sea birds along the coast. Birdwatchers can spot a variety of species in different habitats.
