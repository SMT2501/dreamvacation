Table Mountain

Table Mountain, situated in Cape Town, South Africa, is an iconic landmark and one of the New7Wonders of nature. Table Mountain is renowned for its distinctive flat-topped silhouette, visible from various parts of Cape Town. It is a symbol of the city and a must-see for visitors.

Explore All Corners of South Africa

Sunset Views:

Visit Table Mountain during sunset for a magical experience as the sun sets over the Atlantic Ocean, casting a warm glow on the city and surrounding landscapes.

Fauna:

While exploring the mountain, you may encounter dassies (rock hyraxes), lizards, and various bird species. The endemic Table Mountain ghost frog is also found in the area.

Photography Opportunities:

Table Mountain provides numerous photo opportunities, whether capturing the cityscape, the mountain itself, or the unique flora and fauna.

Discover SA Top Travel Destinations

Table Mountain is not just a geological marvel but a natural treasure offering a range of activities for nature lovers, hikers, photographers, and those seeking panoramic views of Cape Town and its surroundings. For thrill-seekers, there's an option to experience the world's highest commercial abseil from the top of Table Mountain, providing an adrenaline-pumping adventure. On clear nights, Table Mountain offers an excellent vantage point for stargazing. The absence of city lights enhances the visibility of stars and celestial phenomena. For the more adventurous, Table Mountain offers a network of hiking trails, varying in difficulty. Popular routes include Platteklip Gorge and India Venster, each providing unique perspectives of the mountain and city below.
